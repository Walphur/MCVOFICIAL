    Tournament Rules

    01 Team Requirements

    • 5 main players per team.
    • One captain responsible for communication.
    • No roster changes without admin approval.

    02 Mandatory Rules

    • Record all rounds when requested.
    • Screen share during reviews.
    • Stay in assigned voice channels.

    03 Fair Play

    • No cheats, scripts, macros or exploits.
    • Suspicious behavior triggers investigation.
    • Admin decisions are final.

    04 Match Format

    • BO1 / BO3 / BO5 announced per stage.
    • Spawn or side defined by organizers.
    • Late teams may lose by forfeit.

    05 Technical Issues

    • Report issues immediately.
    • Reconnect time is limited.
    • Admins decide pause or rematch.

    06 Reviews

    • One review request per match.
    • Clips are primary evidence.
    • Requests must be made quickly.

    07 Prize

    • Prize paid via PayPal.
    • Captain distributes to team.
    • MCV is not responsible after payment.

    08 Communication

    • Official announcements are in Discord.
    • Only admin messages are official.
    • Teams must follow match calls.

    Fair Play Notice: MCV puede descalificar equipos por cheats, boosting, scripts o incumplimiento de reglas. Traé skill, no excusas.
