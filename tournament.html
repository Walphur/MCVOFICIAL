Know the protocol.

Events Rules

01 Team Requirements

  • — 5 main players (no changes without admin approval).
  • — Optional substitutes (if allowed).
  • — One designated captain — responsible for communication and prize distribution.

02 Mandatory Rules

  • — Record ALL rounds (full clips required).
  • — Screen sharing during ALL matches.
  • — Must be in the official MCV Discord.
  • — Stay in your assigned voice channel.
  • — No external voice communication allowed.

03 Fair Play

  • — No cheats, hacks, scripts or exploits.
  • — Suspicious behavior = investigation.
  • — Admin decisions are FINAL.

04 Match Format

  • — Format: BO1 / BO3 / BO5 (announced per round).
  • — System: Single Elimination or Groups + Playoffs.
  • — Side / Spawn: defined by the organizers when needed.

05 Disconnections & Issues

  • — Technical issues must be reported immediately.
  • — Reconnect time: limited per match — defined by admins.
  • — Failure to return = possible round loss or forfeit.

06 Match Review System

  • — Each team can request 1 review per match.
  • — Time limit: up to 10 minutes after the match.
  • — Clips will be used as main evidence.

07 Prize Distribution

  • — Paid via PayPal to the team captain.
  • — The captain is responsible for distributing the prize among the team.
  • — MCV is NOT responsible once the payment has been sent.

08 Official Communication

  • — All announcements are made via the MCV Discord.
  • — Only messages from admins are considered official.

09 Registration

  • — Sign up via the official registration form on this site.
  • — Limited slots available — 30 teams max.
